Pizza

pizza is a savory dish of talian origin consisting of usually round, flattened base of leaved wheat-based dough topped with tomato paste and cheese and . You can put other types of toppings like pinapple, meat, chicken, olives, onions, etc. it is baked in a chimney structure aka a firewood oven a small pizza is called pizzetta and a person who makes pizza is called a pizzailolo
